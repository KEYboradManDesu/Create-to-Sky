##    Ore Tweaker - Information   ##

Add Ore Configs to the data folder to tweak the world generation
By default the mod and its files mimics vanilla world generation settings.
Check out more info on the wiki at https://github.com/EwyBoy/OreTweaker/wiki

Bugs can be reported here at https://github.com/EwyBoy/OreTweaker/issues

Enjoy and have fun!
